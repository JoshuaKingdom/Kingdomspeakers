About Me

Hi, I’m Joshua. I am passionate about community, inclusiveness, and safe spaces for everyone. I turned my passion into purpose by completing certifications and becoming a Leadership Coach, D.E.I. Ambassador, Community Advocate, and Motivational Speaker. I have personal and professional experience helping leaders, teams, and organizations evolve to their next level of growth. With my knowledge of creativity, determination, and collaboration, I’ve had the opportunity to lead cultural lunches and learns, organize safe space panels, plan educational virtual and in-person events, launch training programs, and be a Guest Speaker at corporate events to discuss various topics such as representation, intersectionality, and LGBTQIA leadership. I walked alongside senior leadership, supporting them with effectively updating onboarding and hiring processes to be more inclusive.

Projects

  • Cultural Lunches & Learns: Organized and facilitated monthly cultural lunches and learns, where employees could share and learn about diverse cultures and traditions. This initiative promoted cultural awareness and inclusivity within the organization.
  • Safe Space Panels: Planned and hosted safe space panels, providing a platform for open and honest discussions on topics such as mental health, LGBTQIA issues, and diversity in the workplace. These panels fostered a supportive and inclusive environment for all participants.
  • Educational Events: Coordinated a series of educational events, both virtual and in-person, covering a range of topics including representation, intersectionality, and leadership. These events aimed to educate and inspire attendees to embrace diversity and inclusivity.
  • Inclusive Training Programs: Developed and launched comprehensive training programs focused on diversity, equity, and inclusion. These programs equipped employees with the knowledge and skills to create a more inclusive workplace.
